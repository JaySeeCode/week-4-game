Crystals Collector

Welcome to the Crystals Collector Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below, each one with an individual value. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of point, that is, the value of the crystal, to your total score.

Your goal is to match the number you were given at the start of the game.

If you can do that, you win. However, you lose if your total score goes above the random number Understood?

Oh, by the way, the value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Pay attention to how the total score changes. Also, you might want to review your basic math...

One last thing... Every time you start a new game, the values of each crystal will be reset.

Good luck!
